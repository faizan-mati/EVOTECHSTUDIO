Frequently Asked Questions

Get answers to common questions about our process

How does your quote process work?

After you submit your project details through our quote form, our team will carefully review your requirements. Within 48 hours, we'll provide you with a detailed proposal that includes project scope, timeline, and pricing. We'll also schedule a consultation call to discuss your project in more detail.

What information do you need to provide an accurate quote?

The more details you provide, the more accurate our quote will be. Important information includes project objectives, desired features and functionality, design preferences, timeline constraints, and budget expectations. Don't worry if you don't have all the details yet—we can help guide you through the discovery process.

How long is my quote valid?

Our quotes are typically valid for 30 days from the date of issue. This gives you sufficient time to review the proposal and make your decision. If you need more time or if your project requirements change, please let us know, and we'll be happy to update your quote accordingly.

Do you require a deposit to start work?

Yes, we typically require a 50% deposit to begin work on your project. This allows us to allocate resources and commit to your timeline. The remaining payment is usually divided into milestone payments throughout the project or due upon completion, depending on the project scope and timeline.

Can you work with my existing design or brand guidelines?

Absolutely! We're experienced in working with established brand guidelines and existing designs. Please share any brand assets, style guides, or design preferences when submitting your quote request, and we'll ensure that our work aligns perfectly with your brand identity.
