Privacy Policy Overview

Last Updated: May 20, 2025

At EvoTech Studio, we take your privacy seriously. This Privacy Policy explains how we collect, use, disclose, and safeguard your information when you visit our website or use our services. Please read this policy carefully to understand our practices regarding your personal data.

Information We Collect

Personal Information

When you use our services, we may collect personal information such as your name, email address, phone number, company name, and job title. This information is collected when you fill out our contact forms, request a quote, or subscribe to our newsletter.

Usage Data

We do not collect any technical information about your visits to our website, such as IP addresses, browser types, operating systems, pages viewed, or timestamps. Your privacy is important to us, and we are committed to providing a secure and anonymous browsing experience.

Cookies

We do not use cookies or any similar tracking technologies. Your browsing experience on our website is completely private, and we do not track or store any information about your visit.

How We Use Your Information

Communication

To respond to your inquiries and provide you with information about our services

Improvement

To improve our website, services, and customer experience

Marketing

To send periodic emails about news, updates, and promotions (with opt-out options)

Security

To protect our website and services from unauthorized access or fraud

Data Protection

We implement appropriate security measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. However, no method of transmission over the internet or electronic storage is 100% secure, so we cannot guarantee absolute security.

Encryption

All sensitive data transmission is encrypted using SSL technology

Access Control

Limited access to personal information only to authorized personnel

Monitoring

Regular security assessments and system monitoring

Third-Party Disclosure

We do not sell, trade, or otherwise transfer your personal information to outside parties without your consent, except in the following circumstances:

01

Service Providers

We may share your information with trusted third parties who assist us in operating our website, conducting our business, or providing services to you.

02

Legal Requirements

We may disclose information when required by law or to protect our rights, privacy, safety, or property.

03

Business Transfers

If EvoTech Studio is involved in a merger, acquisition, or sale of assets, your information may be transferred as part of that transaction.

Your Rights

You have certain rights regarding your personal information. These include:

Access

You can request to see what personal data we hold about you

Correction

You can request that we correct any inaccurate information

Deletion

You can request that we delete your personal information

Opt-Out

You can opt-out of marketing communications at any time
